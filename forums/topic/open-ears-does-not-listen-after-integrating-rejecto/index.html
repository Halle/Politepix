    Halle Winkler
    Politepix

    You can see if Rejecto is just overzealously detecting Rejecto hypotheses and rejecting them if you set [self.languageModelGenerator deliverRejectedSpeechInHypotheses:TRUE] after initializing LanguageModelGenerator. If that is the case you can either turn down the weight or exclude some Rejecto phonemes to reduce the rejection effect. If you’d like to troubleshoot it more, please turn on verbosePocketsphinx and show the complete session log.
